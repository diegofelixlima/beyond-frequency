The review highlights several relationships between elements that characterize Dilara Findikoglu's Fall 2025 collection. These include:

1. **Empowerment and Autonomy**: The collection emphasizes women's empowerment and the importance of defining one's own agenda, reflecting a strong narrative around bodily autonomy and resistance against societal constraints.

2. **Chaos and Control**: The collection explores the theme of chaos, both in the world and within the self, while simultaneously advocating for taking back control. This duality is expressed through the design elements, such as haphazard layering and the use of materials that convey toughness.

3. **Traditional vs. Modern Interpretations**: Corsets, traditionally seen as restrictive, are reinterpreted as armor, symbolizing strength and protection. This shift in perspective connects historical fashion elements with contemporary themes of resilience.

4. **Art and Activism**: The collection serves as a form of artistic expression that addresses broader societal issues, suggesting that fashion can be a medium for activism and change. The designer's narrative intertwines with the craftsmanship, as seen in the collaboration with artisans to create meaningful pieces.

5. **Materiality and Metaphor**: The use of materials like snakeskin and molded leather not only elevates the aesthetic but also serves as metaphors for transformation and renewal, reinforcing the collection's themes of shedding the old and embracing new identities.

Overall, the relationships between these elements create a cohesive narrative that speaks to the designer's vision and the current state of the world, positioning her work as both a fashion statement and a commentary on societal issues.
