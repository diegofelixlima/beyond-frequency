The dominant trends in Simone Rocha's Fall 2025 collection include:

1. **Slow Fashion**: Emphasizing a slow and steady approach to fashion, contrasting with the fast-paced nature of the industry.
2. **Timelessness and Evolution**: The collection reframes the concept of "timeless" by "distilling the past for the present," incorporating familiar silhouettes and styles from Rocha's back catalogue.
3. **Feminine and Playful Elements**: The use of hyper-feminine dresses, A-line and dropped hem designs, and playful embellishments like pearls and fur.
4. **Occasion-led Womenswear**: Focus on creating pieces suitable for specific occasions, with a visual stable of familiar silhouettes.
5. **Textural and Material Experimentation**: Incorporation of materials like fur and denim, with unique styling elements such as harnesses and ribbon cinching.
6. **Cultural References**: The collection includes clever references to Aesop’s fable and nostalgic elements, such as chain jewelry and locks, evoking a sense of storytelling.

Overall, the collection showcases a blend of nostalgia, femininity, and a thoughtful approach to design.
