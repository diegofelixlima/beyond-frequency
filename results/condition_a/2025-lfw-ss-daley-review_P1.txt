The dominant trends in the S.S. Daley Fall 2025 collection include:

1. **Joyful Exploration of Craft Techniques**: The collection showcases new craft techniques, translating artistic inspirations into wearable fashion.
2. **Expansion of Womenswear**: There is a continued and confident expansion into the womenswear category, with a wide range of pieces included.
3. **Artistic Inspiration from Scottish Culture**: The collection draws inspiration from the Scottish Colourists, incorporating bright colors and bold designs that reflect Scottish life and culture.
4. **Reconsideration of Classic British Dress Codes**: The collection features a modern take on classic British styles, blending traditional elements with contemporary silhouettes.
5. **Playful and Quirky Elements**: The use of printed shirts, wooden beads, and jacquard knits adds a playful touch to the collection.
6. **Mixing Silhouettes**: There is a creative juxtaposition of different styles, such as pairing preppy blazers with printed leggings and quilted jackets with ballooning skirts. 

Overall, the collection reflects a blend of artistic expression, cultural homage, and innovative styling.
