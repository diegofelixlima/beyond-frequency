The dominant trends in Dilara Findikoglu's Fall 2025 collection include:

1. **Empowerment and Autonomy**: The collection emphasizes women's empowerment and the importance of defining one's own agenda, reflecting a strong narrative around bodily autonomy and resistance against societal constraints.

2. **Chaos and Control**: The theme of chaos is prevalent, with a focus on taking back control and redefining personal narratives.

3. **Layering and Textures**: The use of haphazard layering of materials such as mesh, lingerie, and strict tailoring showcases a mix of textures and styles.

4. **Reframing Traditional Elements**: Corsets are reinterpreted as armor rather than symbols of restriction, using natural fibers instead of traditional materials.

5. **Artisanal Collaboration**: The collection features a partnership with leather artisans to create unique pieces, highlighting craftsmanship and collaboration.

6. **Metaphorical Use of Materials**: The use of materials like snakeskin serves as a metaphor for transformation and renewal.

7. **Nuanced Messaging**: The collection incorporates layered messages that reflect the designer's perspective on the current state of the world, blending fashion with social commentary.
