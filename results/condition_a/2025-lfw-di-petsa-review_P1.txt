The dominant trends in the Di Petsa Fall 2025 collection include:

1. **Wet-Look Technique**: This has become a signature style for Dimitra Petsa, reworked each season with new fabrications.
2. **Storytelling through Folklore**: The collection draws on Greek heritage, specifically folklore tales, with this season focusing on Eros.
3. **Exploration of Light and Dark**: The collection presents a thematic battle between light and dark, represented through elements like the vampire motif and the focus on desire.
4. **Innovative Use of Textures**: There is an emphasis on tactile and structured styles, with a suggestion to explore knitwear and figure-flattering silhouettes.
5. **Animal Patterns**: A new approach to incorporating animal patterns, moving away from traditional prints like leopard or zebra.
6. **Focus on Key Body Areas**: The collection highlights the chest and crotch as centers of pleasure, emotion, and power, reflecting a subversive take on desire.

Overall, the collection combines a strong aesthetic with thematic depth, exploring complex ideas around desire and duality.
