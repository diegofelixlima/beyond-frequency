The dominant trends in the Roksanda Fall 2025 collection include:

1. **Sculptural Silhouettes**: The collection features sculptural dresses with complex, asymmetrical shapes that echo the work of the referenced woman sculptor, Barlow.

2. **Textural Variety**: There is a strong emphasis on texture, with richly textured weaves and oversized, shimmering paillettes, as well as colorful, shaggy fringe on dresses.

3. **Playful Material Palette**: The use of a playful and sophisticated material palette that incorporates everyday materials in an extravagant manner is a key trend.

4. **Blurring of Categories**: The collection further blurs the boundaries between dresses and tailoring, showcasing a mix of both styles.

5. **Artistic Inspiration**: The collection draws heavily from artistic influences, particularly from the work of a woman artist, emphasizing the intersection of banality and extravagance.

These trends reflect a creative and innovative approach to fashion, focusing on rethinking beauty and celebrating unique artistic expressions.
