The dominant trends in Jawara Alleyne's Fall 2025 collection include:

1. **Maximalist Construction**: The collection features a meticulous method of constructing garments that embody a maximalist viewpoint without appearing overtly so.

2. **Radical Repurposing**: There is a strong emphasis on repurposing and rethinking clothing, with designs that incorporate elements like splicing, shredding, and layering.

3. **Innovative Fabric Use**: The collection utilizes a deeper range of fabrics and graphic prints, showcasing thoughtful experiments in construction.

4. **Layering and Reworking**: The designer's method includes layering and reworking garments, such as shirts flipped upside down and tailored shorts worn over flared jeans.

5. **Imperfection with Impact**: The collection embraces imperfection, moving away from traditional tailoring like perfectly cut pants or cuffed hems, which aligns with a new generation's approach to personal style. 

These trends reflect a focus on sustainability, creativity, and a departure from conventional fashion norms.
